Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how AniRoll (“AniRoll,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or use aniroll.co.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as whether you are logged in.

2. How AniRoll uses cookies

AniRoll uses cookies only for essential purposes, such as authentication, session management, security, and keeping the service working properly. We do not currently use advertising cookies, marketing cookies, retargeting cookies, social media tracking cookies, or analytics cookies.

Type Purpose Required?
Authentication and session cookies Used to keep you logged in, maintain your session, and allow access to protected parts of AniRoll. Yes
Security and infrastructure cookies May be used by hosting, DNS, or security providers to protect the service, route traffic, prevent abuse, or keep the website available. Yes

3. Third-party services

AniRoll uses third-party services to operate the platform. These services may process technical information necessary to provide the service.

  • AniList: used for account login and authentication through AniList OAuth.
  • Railway: used to host AniRoll.
  • Cloudflare: used for DNS and email routing, and may provide security or infrastructure-related functionality.

4. No advertising or tracking cookies

We do not currently use cookies for targeted advertising, behavioral tracking, retargeting, affiliate tracking, or social media advertising.

5. Cookie consent

Because AniRoll currently uses only essential cookies, we do not ask for cookie consent for those cookies. Essential cookies are necessary for the service to function and cannot be disabled through AniRoll.

6. Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you block essential cookies, parts of AniRoll may stop working, including login, authentication, and protected pages.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if AniRoll changes how it uses cookies or adds new services. When we update this page, we will revise the “Last updated” date above.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at privacy@aniroll.co.

If AniRoll later adds analytics, advertising, embedded social widgets, or other non-essential tracking tools, this policy should be updated and a consent banner may be required.
